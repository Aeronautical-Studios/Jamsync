Installation and Usage

The Jamhub client is a single, small (~15MB) binary. To install, just download the client above and put it somewhere on your PATH.

See the help text for the `jam` binary for up-to-date descriptions of the commands.

Contact zach@jamhub.dev or reach out on Discord if you need any assistance!
